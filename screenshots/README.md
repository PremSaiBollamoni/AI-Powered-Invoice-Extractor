## Required Screenshots

Please take the following screenshots and save them in this directory:

### 1. Dashboard (dashboard.png)
**What to capture:**
- Main upload interface
- "AI Invoice Extractor" title
- Upload area with drag & drop zone
- Three feature cards (AI Powered, Fast, Accurate)

**How to take:**
1. Open http://localhost:3000
2. Make sure no invoice is uploaded (fresh page)
3. Take a full-page screenshot
4. Save as `dashboard.png`

---

### 2. AI Processing (ai-processing.png)
**What to capture:**
- Progress indicator showing percentage
- Loading spinner
- "Processing Invoice..." message
- Progress bar animation

**How to take:**
1. Upload a PDF invoice
2. Quickly take a screenshot while it's processing (within 2-3 seconds)
3. Capture the progress bar at around 50-70%
4. Save as `ai-processing.png`

---

### 3. Extracted Invoice (extracted-invoice.png)
**What to capture:**
- Summary cards (Invoice #, Date, Vendor, Total)
- Line items table with data
- Financial summary section
- Export buttons (Excel & CSV)

**How to take:**
1. After successful extraction, scroll to show all sections
2. Take a full-page screenshot showing:
   - Top: Summary cards
   - Middle: Line items table
   - Bottom: Financial summary
3. Save as `extracted-invoice.png`

---

### 4. Activity Log (activity-log.png)
**What to capture:**
- Activity log modal/popup
- List of operations with timestamps
- Success/failure indicators
- Different operation types (upload, extraction, export)

**How to take:**
1. Click "Activity Log" button in the header
2. Make sure you have at least 3-4 log entries
3. Take a screenshot of the log modal
4. Save as `activity-log.png`

---

## Screenshot Specifications

- **Format:** PNG (preferred) or JPG
- **Width:** 1200-1600px (for good quality)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or natural browser window
- **Quality:** High resolution, clear text
